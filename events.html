Events

Craft Night

Come join us for craft night! Explore your imagination and creativity with friends and family. Different projects every week, we never repeat. All ages welcome!

Trivia

Test your knowledge at our TRIVIA Night! We challenge you to try and top Mr. Gray himself (and your beer). Bet you can’t!

Marketplace

Our marketplace features local vendors, a popup bar, live local music, and fresh produce. Come try out our top 4 recipes prepared by Mrs. Gray and the staff here at Gray Ridge Brewing.

Bingo Night

BINGO! You’ve come to the right place. Hosting up to 60 participants per night, winners receive gift cards, packs of Gray Ridge beer, and brewery tours for 2.

Board Game Night

Monopoly, Game of Life, Candy Land, Pictionary, Shoots and Ladders, our Board Game Night has it all. Bring your neighbours, friends and family. All ages welcome!

BYO Vinyl

Bring Your Own Vinyl is one of our most popular event nights. Playing... well, whatever you bring!

Live Music Thursdays

Sip a cold one at our Live Music Thursdays. Sit back and enjoy the show, or be in the show! Send us an email located on our contact page linked with a video of your performance.

Paint Night

Paint, beer and friends, the perfect combo. Hosted by local artist, Devyn Carter, expand you creativity and brush skills at our paint night!
